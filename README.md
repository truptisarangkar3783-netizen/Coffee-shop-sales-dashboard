# Coffee-shop-sales-dashboard
Dynamic Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard in Excel providing insights on revenue, product performance, and peak sales trends.

☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Excel)
🔍 Overview

This dashboard represents Coffee Shop Sales data analysis using Excel, focusing on transactions, revenue, profit, and category performance across different years, months, and cities.

🎯 Objective
Analyze total transactions, quantity sold, revenue, and profit
Understand category-wise and city-wise performance
Track monthly and weekday transaction trends
Compare yearly revenue contribution

📊 Dashboard Features
KPI Cards:
Total Transactions: 9971
Total Quantity Sold: 14117
Total Revenue: ₹46,374
Total Profit: ₹16,231
Filters Available:
Year (2021, 2022, 2023)
Month (Jan–Dec)
City (Austin, Chicago, Los Angeles, New York, Seattle)
Visuals Included:
Category-wise Transaction (Tea, Coffee, Snacks, etc.)
Weekday Transaction Count
Yearly Revenue % (2021, 2022, 2023)
Revenue by Category
Monthly Transactions (Year-wise comparison)
Revenue % by City
Monthly Profit and Revenue comparison

🛠️ Tools Used
Microsoft Excel
Pivot Tables
Slicers
Charts & Graphs

💡 Insights
Friday has the highest number of transactions
Desserts category shows the highest transaction value
Revenue contribution is almost equally divided across 2021, 2022, and 2023 (~33% each)
Chicago contributes the highest revenue percentage among cities
Monthly transactions remain consistent with slight variation across months
Profit and revenue follow a similar trend throughout the year

🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard provides a clear and structured view of sales performance across different dimensions like time, category, and location, helping in better business understanding and decision-making.
